What is Blockchain?
Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers in a secure and transparent way. It ensures data integrity by making it nearly impossible to alter past transactions without altering all subsequent blocks, requiring network consensus.

Q) What is a Smart Contract?
A smart contract is a self-executing contract with the terms of the agreement directly written into lines of code. It automatically executes and enforces actions when predefined conditions are met, without requiring intermediaries.
